Tournament Info

The OnBrand Gaming League is proud to host no-cost tournaments for gaming enthusiasts. Have you spent years honing your gaming skills and feel you are ready to put those skills to the test in a no-holds-barred team v team or 1v1 bracket in front of a live audience? Want to know who is the best of the best of your gaming friends? Are you ready to get out of the house for a change? Register for a tournament today!

Here are the details:

  • Must be present to play. No remote participation
  • First place will receive a trophy and bragging rights (no cash prizes)
  • Only players are required to register, spectators are not
  • Minimun age limit to compete is 15. There is no maximum age limit to participate. There will be a <18 y/o bracket and an 18+ y/o bracket whenever possible.
  • Registration for competing teams will be no-cost
  • Admission for spectators is no-cost
  • Competing team members may need to bring thier own console/pc (BYOC) (see details below)
  • Event staff will provide monitors, headphones (with mics), and ethernet cables to connect to the LAN
  • Events will be offline and we encourge players to download the most current system and game updates prior to the tournament
  • Use of mods or cheating of any kind will result in disqualificaiton
  • Registration will close 1 week prior to the event
  • The number of participating teams will be limited and may vary in each tournament. Bracket seats will be awarded on a first come/first serve basis. Competing teams are encouraged to REGISTER EARLY to guarentee a spot in the bracket!



Don’t want to play? That’s ok, it’s free to watch as well. Come support your local teams as they battle it out on the big screen. Feel every shot and every victory thanks to our outstanding audio system. If you need a break from the tournament we offer other activites as well:

  • Cornhole
  • Giant Jenga
  • Hangout with your friends
  • Concessions and refreshments (proceeds fund more events like this)
  • Bring an insturment and jam outdoors
  • Get out the house for once, and brag to your friends about it
  • Let us know how to improve future events
