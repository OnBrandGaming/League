Tournament Info

The OnBrand Gaming League is proud to host tournaments for gaming enthusiasts. Have you spent years honing your gaming skills and feel you are ready to put those skills to the test in a no-holds-barred team v team or 1v1 bracket in front of a live audience? Want to know who is the best of the best of your gaming friends? Are you ready to get out of the house for a change? Register for a tournament today!

Here are the details:

  • Must be present to play. No remote participation.
  • Prize pool will be a 70/30 split for top two. First Place will also receive a trophy and of course bragging rights.
  • Only players are required to register, spectators are not. Entry fee for players will be $10 per player.
  • Competing team members may waive registration fees if they provide a personal console/pc for shared used throughout the event. Individuals providing personal equipment for tournament use will be required to sign an equipment loss/damage waiver. (First come first serve basis, the number of extra consoles needed will vary by tournament. Contact tournament staff if you wish to participate in this opportunity).
  • Minimum age limit to compete is 15. There is no maximum age limit to participate. There will be a < 18 y/o bracket and an 18+ y/o bracket whenever possible.
  • Registration will be finalized at the event.
  • The number of participating teams will be limited and may vary in each tournament. Bracket seats will be awarded on a first come/first serve basis. Competing teams and individuals are encouraged to REGISTER EARLY to guarentee a spot in the bracket!
  • All competitors will be required to sign and return an acknowledgement of tournament rules.
  • Competitors under the age of 18 will also need signatures (permission) from legal parent/guardian(s) when completing tournament forms. Guardians are not required to attend the event but are very welcome.
  • Competitors should check in 30 minutes prior to start of the event.
  • Admission for spectators is $1 and can be paid in cash at the door. The $1 admission fee does not apply to Players, only spectators.
  • Event staff will provide consoles, monitors, headphones (with mics), and controllers for competition.
  • Events will be offline and will not be streamed.
  • Players who use unapproved software or hardware mods or cheat in any way will be subject to disqualification at the discretion of event staff. Registration fees will not be reimbursed to individuals disqualified regardless of grounds for disqualification.
  • Alcohol and drug use is strictly prohibited at all gaming events. Players who violate this rule will be disqualified from tournament play.



Don’t want to play? That’s ok, admission is only $1 so come support your local teams as they battle it out on the big screen. Feel every shot and every victory thanks to our outstanding audio system. If you need a break from the tournament we offer other activities as well:

  • Cornhole
  • Giant Jenga
  • Concessions and refreshments (proceeds fund more events like this)
  • Bring an instrument and jam outdoors
  • Get out the house for once, and brag to your friends about it
  • Let us know how to improve future events
